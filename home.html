Our Amazing Activities

Experience the ultimate in fitness, fun, and entertainment with our diverse range of activities

Dance Studio

Join our energetic dance team! Learn hip-hop, contemporary, salsa, and more. Professional instructors guide you through choreography that'll make you move like a pro. Perfect for all skill levels from beginners to advanced dancers.

Boxing Arena

Step into our state-of-the-art boxing facility! Train with certified coaches, improve your technique, and get in the best shape of your life. Heavy bags, speed bags, and full ring available for sparring sessions.

Sports Complex

Multi-purpose courts for basketball, volleyball, badminton, and more! Join leagues, participate in tournaments, or just have fun with friends. Professional-grade equipment and facilities await you.

Game Room Floor 1

Classic arcade games, pinball machines, air hockey, pool tables, and vintage consoles! Relive your childhood or discover retro gaming. Perfect for parties, dates, or just unwinding after a workout.

Game Room Floor 2

Next-generation gaming experience! VR stations, latest console games, racing simulators, and high-tech entertainment. Compete in esports tournaments or explore virtual worlds with cutting-edge technology.

Recreation Zone

Relax and socialize in our recreation areas! Lounge spaces, cafeteria, smoothie bar, and wellness corner. Take a break between activities and connect with fellow members in a comfortable environment.
